# Sonar Seed

**The complete influencer gifting workflow, built entirely inside Shopify.**

Sonar Seed is the Shopify-native platform designed for brands that have outgrown spreadsheets but want to avoid the high cost and complexity of enterprise influencer tools. By living entirely inside the Shopify admin, it automates the full "gifting loop" - from sending products to tracking sales and paying commissions - without requiring a separate platform login.

[Sonar Seed Website](https://www.sonarseed.com) | [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com) | [Help Center](https://www.sonarseed.com/support)

-----

## What is Sonar Seed?

Most Shopify brands manage influencer gifting through a messy combination of DMs, manual draft orders, and spreadsheets. Sonar Seed replaces this fragmented process with a single, automated workflow that stays 100% within your Shopify dashboard.

### The Gifting Loop

1.  **Gift the Product:** Create a campaign and ship products via Shopify draft orders in seconds.
2.  **Assign a Code:** Automatically generate unique, synced Shopify discount codes for every ambassador.
3.  **Track & Pay:** Tie every sale back to the specific influencer and review commissions before they pay out.

-----

## Key Features

  * **Shopify Native:** No separate logins or context switching. Manage your creators where you manage your store.
  * **Frictionless Onboarding:** Influencers receive a "Magic Link" to a branded portal. No accounts, no passwords, no barrier to entry.
  * **Klaviyo Integration:** Sync your ambassador lists to Klaviyo to send recruitment and performance emails using your own brand templates.
  * **White-Label Experience:** Every creator touchpoint carries your brand’s identity, not ours.
  * **Margin Protection:** Review and approve every commission manually to ensure you only pay for genuine, influencer-driven conversions.

-----

## Integration & Tech Stack

Sonar Seed is built to enhance your existing Shopify stack, not replace it.

  * **Platform:** Exclusive to Shopify & Shopify Plus.
  * **Email:** Deep integration with Klaviyo for lifecycle automated flows.
  * **Data:** Real-time syncing with Shopify’s native Discount and Order APIs for 100% attribution accuracy.
